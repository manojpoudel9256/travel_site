Before you write

Quick Answers

Do I need a visa for Nepal?

Most nationalities get a tourist visa on arrival at Kathmandu (Tribhuvan) airport — bring USD cash and a passport photo. We'll send a full checklist once your trip is booked.

How fit do I need to be for Base Camp?

If you can comfortably hike 5–6 hours a day on hills for a week, you're ready. It's about endurance and acclimatization, not technical skill — our itineraries build in rest days by design.

Is travel insurance required?

Yes — we require insurance covering high-altitude trekking and helicopter evacuation. For expeditions, coverage must extend to your peak's summit altitude. We're happy to recommend providers.

What's the payment process?

A deposit secures your dates and permits; the balance is due before departure. We accept bank transfer and major cards, and provide a formal invoice for your records.
